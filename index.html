How to Publish Your Google Sheet

Step-by-step Setup

Your sheet must be published as CSV so this app can read it.

  1. Open your Google Sheet in a browser.
  2. Click File → Share → Publish to web.
  3. In the first dropdown, choose the specific sheet tab (e.g. "Sheet1").
  4. In the second dropdown, choose Comma-separated values (.csv).
  5. Click Publish and confirm.
  6. Copy the URL — it looks like:
    https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/YOUR_ID/pub?gid=0&single=true&output=csv
  7. Paste that URL above and tap Sync Google Sheet.

Required Column Headers (Row 1)

Date  Artist(s)  Venue  Attended With  Type  Notes  Setlist

Date: YYYY-MM-DD format.
Artist(s): Multiple artists on separate lines within the cell — the app will display them with commas in list view and on separate lines in detail view.
Venue: Include city and state in the venue name (e.g. "The Sinkhole St. Louis, MO").
Setlist: Leave blank or paste a setlist.fm URL.
